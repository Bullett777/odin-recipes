Chuy's Jalapeno Ranch Dip

Description

This is a copycat recipe for chuy's creamy jalapeno dip. the dip is a mix of sour cream, milk, ranch seasoning, black pepper, salt, garlic powder, and cilantro!

Ingredients

  • sour cream
  • milk
  • ranch seasoning
  • black pepper
  • salt
  • garlic powder
  • cilantro
  • diced pickled jalapeno

Steps

  1. add all the ingredients together in a blender
  2. mix thoroughly until smooth
  3. chill for 4 hours and enjoy
